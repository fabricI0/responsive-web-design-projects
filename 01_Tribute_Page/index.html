    Here's a time line of Andres Mánuel López Obrador:

  • 1953 - Born in Tepetitlán, Tabasco, México
  • 1970 - He begins his political career by joining the Institutional Revolutionary Party (PRI) to support Carlos Pellicer to the Senate of the Republic.
  • 1977 - He becomes state delegate of the National Indigenous Institute (INI).
  • 1980 - Married Rocío Beltrán Medina, whom he met at UAT a year earlier, while teaching sociology at the School of Education Sciences.
  • 1984 - Traveled to the Federal District to assume the Directorate of Social Promotion of the National Consumer Institute and was a teacher at the Institute of political formation of the PRI.
  • 1986 - Present his 'tesis' to get majored in Political Science and Public Administration at the National Autonomous University of Mexico (UNAM)
  • 1988 - Joined the Democratic Current, a faction of the Institutional Revolutionary Party that opposed the traditional method of selecting the presidential candidate.
  • 1989 - The National Democratic Front consolidates forming the Party of the Democratic Revolution (PRD) and López Obrador becomes president of the party in the state of Tabasco.
  • 1994 - It returned to be candidate to the governorship of Tabasco in the state elections of 1994, endorsed by the PRD being defeated by the PRI candidate, Roberto Madrazo Pintado.
  • 1996 - He began his campaign for the presidency of the Party of the Democratic Revolution (PRD), contending with Heberto Castillo and Amalia García. He held the position of president of the PRD from August 2, 1996 to April 10, 1999.
  • 2000 - Assumed the leadership of the Federal District elected for a period of six years. During his tenure he stood out for holding press conferences every day at 6 o'clock in the morning to answer questions from the media.
  • 2006 - Took the protest as candidate of the Coalition for the Good of All and obtained his registration as a presidential candidate before the Federal Electoral Institute.
  • 2012 - At the Teatro Metropólitan, he took office as candidate of the Movimiento Ciudadano party to the Presidency of Mexico in the electoral process of July 1.
  • 2014 - Promoted the refoundation as a political party of the National Regeneration Movement (MORENA), the civic platform that had promoted his candidacy.
  • 2018 - Andrés Manuel López Obrador becomes the first President of the Republic emanating from the left in the history of the México. He arrives thisat the National Palace with the backing of an overwhelming vote that gave him not only the Presidential Band, but an overwhelming majority in the Congress of the Union.
