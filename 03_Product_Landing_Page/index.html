Educational Model

The educational model of the Open and Distance University of Mexico seeks to satisfy the demand for higher education of Mexican society through a flexible curriculum.

Curricular Model

The curricular model of the Open and Distance University of Mexico has a competency-based approach that incorporates the advantages of distance education, enhanced by the use of information and communication technologies, under a flexible operating scheme and democratic access.

Modular Structuring

Each module is made up of serial semesters (1056 hours or 55 credits). Each semester is equivalent to 288 hours (20-25 credits) of learning, for four subjects.The student decides from the second semester how many subjects he wants to study respecting the seriation of each study plan (minimum two, maximum four).
